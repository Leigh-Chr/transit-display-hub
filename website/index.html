Everything you need for transit information

From network configuration to passenger-facing displays, Transit Display Hub handles it all.

Line & Stop Management

Create and manage transit lines, stops, and itineraries from a comprehensive admin dashboard.

Schedule Management

Define timetables with flexible frequency-based and time-based scheduling patterns.

Real-Time Displays

Passenger information kiosks showing live arrivals, departures, and service alerts in real time.

Interactive Network Map

Schematic visualization of your entire transit network with route search and pan & zoom.

Broadcast Messages

Send info, warning, and critical alerts scoped to the whole network, specific lines, or individual stops.

Device Monitoring

Register, configure, and monitor the health of all display devices from a single dashboard.
